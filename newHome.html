Blueprints

you can source online blueprints or let us know if you need a architect, TCH has approved architects that can draw your blueprints from scratch. if you find blueprints online but it's not exactly what you want our architects can use that as a starting point. All plans will be reviewed by TCH and approved by the home owner, TCH and the architect.

When Approved

TCH will estimate the cost of your build. build fee is $1 per square foot, this fee will be removed once the contract as been executed. the builder fee starts at 15% of material and labor to build your home from start to finish. Example: labor $50,000 + $50,000 for material = $100,000 x $0.15 = $15,000 builders fee. the builders fee does not change unless there are change orders made. change order fees have an additional builders fee of 30%.

Payments

After the contract executions (finalized) construction will begin, TCH will be due the 1st of 5 draws (payments) at contract signing

Draws Timeline

  • 1. At execution of contract
  • 2. After Foundation is complete
  • 3. After Home has been framed and blacked in
  • 4. After Trim is complete
  • 5. When Home is finished
